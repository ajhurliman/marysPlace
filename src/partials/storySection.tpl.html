THE STORY

LAUNCH DATE: NOV 16, 2016
DEADLINE TO GIVE: DEC 31, 2016

Tonight, 500 families will sleep outside in Seattle. Mostly single mothers with children, sleeping in places that are cold, wet, and unsafe.

Please join our incredible launch supporters Amazon, Bank of America, Biscuit Bitch, Dick’s Drive-In Restaurants, Downtown Seattle Association, HomeStreet Bank, Lennar Multifamily Communities, Seattle Restaurant Alliance, Starbucks, Seattle Chamber of Commerce, Seattle Women’s Council of Realtors, and Zoots to help Mary's Place raise over $500,000 to open additional shelters in early 2017 to bring as many families inside at night as possible!

With your help, we can make our goal of moving families out of encampments, doorways and cars and into safe, emergency shelter a reality!

  • For every $10,000 we raise, 25 families can get the down payments they need to move in to transitional or permanent housing.
  • $120,000 will provide over 10,000 nights of shelter for families currently sleeping outside.
  • $250,000 & we can fund our ongoing work to provide emergency shelter and services to the 400+ women and children who come to our door every day.
  • $500,000 will pay for a crisis-response night shelter for one year to bring families experiencing homelessness inside to safety, and provide 36,000 additional bed nights for families in need next year (100 Beds)

At Mary’s Place, we empower more than 400 homeless women, children, and families each night to reclaim their lives by providing shelter, nourishment, resources, healing and hope in a safe community.

Help us make the third year of the No Child Sleeps Outside campaign the most successful yet! Together with our sponsors and generous community, we’ve raised over $750,000 the last two years and more than doubled our capacity from 150 shelter beds in 2014, to 400 today. Help us double that number again!

Last year’s success allowed us to open the Mary’s Place Family Center in North Seattle, and the Mary’s Place Guest Rooms shelter in partnership with Amazon in South Lake Union!

At Mary’s Place, a family experiencing homelessness begins their journey into permanent housing. We CAN bring our city’s 500 families inside and out of cars, bus stations, and doorways with YOUR HELP!

We need to do more. TOGETHER we can bring these families – moms, dads, and children – inside to warmth and safety this winter.
